Places Must Visit

dosa hut

i had visited dosa hut in june 2022. the food was amazing and the place was so friendly.

There are days when you just want to have delicious food that doesn’t disappoint. And for days like these, Dosa Hut’s new outlet in Canberra City is your go-to place. As one of Australia’s fastest-growing Indian food chains, Dosa Hut is steadily expanding its footprint, with its latest restaurant in Canberra City. With over 16 branches in less than a decade, we are known to be the pioneers of Dosa culture in Australia. So what is it really that makes us so popular? The fact that we focus on the simple basics, and have a team that is committed to creating the best meal experience at a great value.

review on movies

The Lion King
In the Pride Lands of Africa, a pride of lions rule over the animal kingdom from Pride Rock. King Mufasa, the ruler of the Pride Lands, shows his son, Simba, the Pride Lands and explains to him the responsibilities of kingship and the "circle of life," which connects all living things

Simba and his best friend Nala explore a forbidden elephant graveyard, where they are chased by three hyenas named Shenzi, Banzai, and Ed. Mufasa is alerted by his majordomo, the hornbill Zazu, and rescues the cubs. Though disappointed, Mufasa forgives Simba and explains that the great kings of the past watch over them from the night sky, from which he will one day watch over Simba. Meanwhile, Mufasa's brother Scar visits the hyenas and convinces them to help him overthrow Mufasa in exchange for hunting rights in the Pride Lands

The Avengers: Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

u should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watching Avengers: Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile.After Thanos, an intergalactic warlord, disintegrates half of the universe, the Avengers must reunite and assemble again to reinvigorate their trounced allies and restore balance.
